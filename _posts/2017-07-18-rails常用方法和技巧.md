## 查询


### 按时间范围查询
~~~
User.where(date => 30.days.ago..Time.now)
User.where('created_at < :sixty_days_ago', sixty_days_ago: Time.now - 60.days)
User.where(created_at: Time.current.all_week) # all_day, all_week, all_quarter, all_year
~~~
